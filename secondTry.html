The Wood community offers a wide variety of apartments and vibrant community spaces on both the Ground Floor and First Floor. The First Floor is seamlessly aligned with the sloping courtyard on the west side of the building. The first entrance of the courtyard is on the westside of the apartment blocks. A second entrance to the courtyard was created by making a cut through the building on the northside.

The facade features thoughtful setbacks that not only create inviting outdoor areas but also offer a wide variety of apartments.

The courtyard is designed with height differences which creates interesting spaces with a good overview on the courtyard. The terraces are accessible for everyone because of the ramps in the middle of the courtyard.
