A vacation home designed on a winter dike in the Zwolle Scheller and ‘Oldeneler buitenwaarden’. The front of the house is anchored at the highest point of the dike. The back hovers above the sloping dike due to the use of a steel structure that can absorb both tensile and compressive forces. The design is based on tranquility and simplicity.

The house offers a breathtaking view on the ‘Zwolse buitenwaarden’ immediately upon entering. The roof overhang influences the heat stress in the summer and provides enough sunlight in the winter.

Location: Zwolle, Overijsel

Function: Logies

Area: 64m²
